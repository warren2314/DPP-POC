# Privacy & Data Protection Compliance Checklist

## Personal Data Processing - Direct Identifiers

- [ ] Does your product process names of natural persons?
- [ ] Does your product process home or business addresses of natural persons?
- [ ] Does your product process personal or business phone numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process home or business email addresses?
- [ ] Does your product process other information which identifies individuals in terms of making them directly addressable or contactable?

## Technical Identifiers

- [ ] Does your product process IP addresses?
- [ ] Does your product process any device numbers such as the media access control (MAC) address?
- [ ] Does your product process identifiers like customer number, supplier number, employee number?
- [ ] Does your product process user ID numbers?
- [ ] Does your product write identifiers like the user id to log or trace files?

## Financial & Legal Identifiers

- [ ] Does your product process vehicle license plate numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process vehicle identity numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process membership numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process bank account numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process credit card numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process medical record numbers?
- [ ] Does your product process health plan benefit numbers?

## Biometric & Visual Data

- [ ] Does your product process biometric information?
- [ ] Does your product process biometric identifiers like fingerprints or voice prints?
- [ ] Does your product process full face photographic images or any comparable images which allow the identification of natural persons?

## Temporal & Location Data

- [ ] Does your product process dates directly related to natural persons like date of birth, medical facility admission and discharge dates, entry dates, termination dates?
- [ ] Does your product process geo location data of devices which can be linked to natural persons like mobile phones or cars?

## Indirect Identifiers & Profiling

- [ ] Does your product process unique information like browser footprints which may lead to an identification of a natural person?
- [ ] Does your product process other information which contains identifiers that enable an indirect connection to an individual?
- [ ] Does your product process information that contains or is associated with a characteristic or a set of attributes which distinguishes a natural person from other natural persons?

## Communications Data

- [ ] Does your product process voice call content?
- [ ] Does your product process recorded phone calls?
- [ ] Does your product process other information which is about an individual natural person?

## Special Categories & Sensitive Processing

- [ ] Does your product offer generic containers in which solutions / customers can process personal data?
- [ ] Will you have any Special Categories of data?
- [ ] Will you perform Automated Decisions?
- [ ] Will you engage in Profiling?
- [ ] Will you Monitor people's movement in a public space (e.g., via video)?
- [ ] Will you have other Sensitive or Embarrassing data (e.g., criminal convictions or arrests)?

## Organizational & Legal Basis

- [ ] Will this product be operated by SAP Fioneer and process personal data, which makes us a Controller?

## Required Privacy Controls & Safeguards

### Consent & Legal Basis
- [ ] Capture explicit consent before collecting personal data, where no other legal ground exists.
- [ ] Customer data is **Not** used for partner's own purpose without contractual consent or legal ground.

### Data Subject Rights
- [ ] Provide a retrieval function which can be used to inform the data subjects about the personal data stored about them.
- [ ] Erasure of personal data is supported.
- [ ] Provide EU Access.

### Logging & Transparency
- [ ] Log read access to sensitive personal data.
- [ ] Changes to personal data can be logged.

### Data Protection Principles
- [ ] Ensure Personal Data Minimization.
- [ ] Allow human intervention for all automated decision processes.

### AI & Algorithmic Safeguards
- [ ] Ensure used AI methods do **Not** result in inaccuracies detrimental to data subjects' rights.
- [ ] Provide transparency when using AI during processing of personal data.
- [ ] Implement safeguards to protect personal data used to train AI model.
